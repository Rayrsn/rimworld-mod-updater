Updater:

Run RimworldModUpdater.exe
If not set already, select your rimworld installation folder by clicking "Open Rimworld folder" in the top left.
Click "Query Mod Files" and wait fof inish checking for outdated mods.
After it's finished querying, you can either select multiple mods and right click to update specific mods, or just click "Update All" to update every mod in the list.


Downloader:

Simply browse to any file/collection page using the embedded browser and click the subscribe button on the page. The mod will be automatically downloaded & dropped into your Mods folder.
Newly downloaded mods will show up in the "Downloaded Mods" tab.
Collection files will not show in "Downloaded Mods" until the entire collection is finished downloading.
